## Overview
<p>My personal portfolio built using the NextJS framework. Originally the idea changed several times, combining it with a shorter timeframe. It took me a long time to complete this project.
At the end of the journey I am happy with the result. There will always be something to change, to improve, but I am happier than ever to have finally completed a decent protfolio site.</p>

## The idea of change.
<p>Dissatisfied with previous iterations of my portfolio, I found myself trapped in a cycle of perpetual refinement, constantly seeking that elusive spark of brilliance. After long time I decided to make my portfolio website look more simple and proffessional. I completely redesign it. I embraced a minimalist aesthetic, drawing inspiration from the elegance of platforms like Apple. I wanted to make full control of a website and ease adding new project and technologies.</p>

- NextJS
